Simple Hot Dog Recipe

Description

This is just a plain hotdog recipe

Ingredients:

  • Hotdog bun
  • Some Franks
  • Various Condiments
  • Optional: American Cheese
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Take a hotdog bun and open it and spread some butter inside. Just a little
  2. Preheat a skillet with a tiny bit of oil and when it's hot enough, take a frank or two and split it open with a knife before placing in the skillet for 5-8 minutes
  3. Toast the bun face down and open inside the skillet until slightly browned
  4. Remove the frank and the bun and put them together
  5. Add some condiments
