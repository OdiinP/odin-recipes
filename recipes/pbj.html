Simple Peanut Butter and Jelly Sanwich

Description

This is just a basic peanut butter and jelly sandwich recipe

Ingredients:

  • Two slices of white or wheat bread
  • Strawberry or Grape Jam
  • Peanut Butter (I prefer Jif)

Steps:

  1. Take two pieces of bread and set them aside for now
  2. Get a jar of Jam and take out as much as you'd want and spread it on one half
  3. Get a jar of peanut butter and take out as much as you'd want and spread it on the other half
  4. Viola!
